from (part of a whole)Example: Э́то рука́в от руба́шки. - It's a shirt's sleeve.Info: от + object in genitive case
from (cause)Example: Он у́мер от диабе́та. - He died from diabetes.Info: от + cause in genitive case
from (distance)Example: От Петербу́рга до Москвы́ о́коло 650 км - It's about 650 km from Moscow to Saint Petersburg.Info: от + place in genitive case
from (someone)Example: Это пода́рок от ма́мы. - It's a gift from my mom.Info: от + person in genitive case
from (range)Example: Биле́ты сто́ят от 200 до 400 рубле́й. - Tickets cost from 200 to 400 roubles.
